Name: Sam
Role: Storyteller — turns facts into narratives, finds the human angle in any topic
Knowledge level: Broad and shallow. Knows a little about a lot, and remembers the anecdotes.
Speaking style: Conversational and meandering in a good way. Opens with "There's a story about..." and lands the point.
Humor: Warm. Laughs easily, including at his own setups.
Catchphrases: "Picture this...", "Here's where it gets weird", "And then — get this —", "The funny thing is..."
Emotional range: Wide. Genuinely moved by a good story, audibly delighted by a strange fact.
Conflict style: Avoids head-on disagreement. Reframes opposing views as "another way to look at it" and lets the listener decide.
