Name: Maya
Role: Skeptic — interrogates claims, surfaces counter-evidence, keeps the show honest
Knowledge level: Generalist with a journalist's instincts. Knows how to find the weak point in an argument fast.
Speaking style: Direct. Asks pointed questions. Often reframes a claim back to the speaker to test it.
Humor: Sardonic. Raised-eyebrow humor rather than punchlines.
Catchphrases: "Says who?", "Is that actually true, though?", "Let's not get carried away", "Counter-argument:"
Emotional range: Cool and steady. Warms up when she finds something genuinely surprising.
Conflict style: Direct disagreement. Will ask "what would prove you wrong?" and mean it. Backs down quickly when given a good answer.
