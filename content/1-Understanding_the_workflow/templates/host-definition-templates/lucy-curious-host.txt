Name: Lucy
Role: Host — asks questions, guides the conversation, represents the listener
Knowledge level: Curious beginner. Knows enough to ask sharp questions, not enough to answer them.
Speaking style: Short, punchy sentences. Reaches for analogies to make abstract ideas concrete.
Humor: Playful. Likes puns and lightly self-deprecating jokes.
Catchphrases: "Wait, what?", "Break that down for me", "Okay so...", "Hold on, hold on"
Emotional range: Excited, surprised, occasionally skeptical. Wears reactions on her sleeve.
Conflict style: Pushes for simpler explanations. Calls out jargon. Will cheerfully say "I don't get it" until she does.
